Instructions

  • Click to add orgin and destination
    1. Click to add two markers and 1st is origin, 2nd is destination.
      PS: You can drag to adjust their location.
    2. Choose travelling mode and 'Lowest Toll'.
    3. Click 'Show Route of added markers' to see details on the right panel.
    4. You can click 'RESET' to delete all markers and routes.
    5. Notice: for this method, if you change the travel mode, you need to click 'Show Route of added markers' to see new route.

  • Find origin and destination by entering names
    1. Enter their names in first two boxes.
    2. Choose travelling mode and 'Lowest Toll'.
    3. You can check route details on the right panel.
    4. You can drag the origin and destination to adjust the route.

  • Find places of a certain type
    1. Hover on 'Category Key Words' to check examples of types.
    2. Enter one keyword in category search box to find places of this type.
    3. Choose one marker as the destination.
    4. Click on the map to add one origin marker.
    5. Click 'Show Route of added markers' to see details on the right panel.
