Instructions

  1. Click 'Get Current Location' or enter your Latitude and Longitude manually.
  2. Select number of trigonometric stations to search.
  3. Select travelling mode.
  4. Select sorting method: by straight distance, time or actual distance
  5. Click 'Show Stations' and check stations in right panel.
    You need to click and unfold the 'Stations' section.
  6. Select one station, check its radio button.
  7. Click 'Display Route' and check route details in folded 'Route' section in the right panel.
