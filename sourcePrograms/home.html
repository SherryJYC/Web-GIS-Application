About

This is a GIS Project produced by

This website is try to provide a GIS application for driving route and search functions related to trig stations in Hong Kong.
In route map, you can find routes by adding markers or search address and explore certain places, such as cafe and parking.
In trig map, you can find the nearby trignometric stations and the route to these places.
Instructions of these maps can be found on the 'HELP' section on their pages.
